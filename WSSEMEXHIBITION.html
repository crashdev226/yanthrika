WORKSHOP, SEMINAR AND EXHIBITION : INTRODUCTION TO RASPBERRY PI & INTERFACING OF VARIOUS SENSORS WITH IT.

A workshop on any of the core instrumentation topics ( PLC, DCS, Robotics and Automation etc..)will be taken by an experienced resource person in the respective domain. After the workshop there will be a seminar presentation by the students of S6 Electronics & Instrumentation on various topics which are closely related with instrumentation engineering. The seminar presentation will be of maximum 1 hour. After the seminar there will be an exhibition, where we are planning to exhibit various instruments, control valves, various sensors and transducers etc. For the participants who registered for the workshop, the seminar and workshop will be free.
