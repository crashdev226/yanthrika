CIRCUIT SETTING COMPETITION

The participants should set the given circuits in the breadboard provided to them. There will be two rounds. Round 1 is the preliminary round. Here easy and simple circuits are given to the contestants. The contestants qualifying the preliminary round will be selected for the final round. The questions in the final round will be tough compared to preliminary. The circuits designed on the breadboard must be in a perfectly organized manner. The one who completes the circuit first will be selected as the winner. Connections made with too lengthy wires and equipment beyond the requirements will be neglected even though the participant completed the circuit first.

Prize money for 1st position : Rs. 1500
Prize money for 2nd position : Rs.1000
Registration fees for each Contestant: Rs 200
Approximate total cost for execution of the event (Including prize money and other expenses) : Rs 3500
